Core Idea

What this project is meant to solve

Today, many organization activities are scattered across calls, spreadsheets, manual records, and disconnected tools. This portal turns that into a structured digital operating system for the entire organization.

Public Trust & Visibility

  • Showcase the mission, departments, projects, and team structure professionally.
  • Make it simple for people to understand what the organization does and how to join.

Member Automation

  • Digitize registration, approvals, payments, receipts, ID cards, and meeting communication.
  • Give each member a clean self-service dashboard.

Admin Efficiency

  • Provide role-based control from national level down to colony and society teams.
  • Reduce repetitive manual work through workflow-driven operations.
Product Scope

The platform is built around three major surfaces

Each surface solves a different part of the journey, but they all run on one shared system and one connected data model.

1. Public Website

  • Home, About, Maharaja Agrasen, Projects, Teams, Contact, Search
  • Department landing pages and organization structure visibility
  • Membership discovery and joining flow

2. Member Portal

  • Login, signup, OTP, dashboard, payments, receipts, digital ID
  • Directory, meetings, agenda, MOM, notifications, donation history
  • Membership application and status tracking

3. Admin Panel

  • Members, approvals, team hierarchy, departments, meetings, ads, CMS
  • Finance tracking, exports, reports, permissions, content operations
  • National to local team-level administration

Architecture

A scalable structure for growth and automation

The recommended architecture separates user experiences while keeping data and business logic centralized. This makes the platform easier to maintain, extend, and scale across states and departments.

Automation Vision

How the system becomes fully automated

Automation is not just a feature here. It is the main value driver of the entire project.

1

User Registers

People join through the public website or member portal with validated details and structured profile data.

2

System Verifies

OTP confirmation, payment confirmation, and admin-ready application data are prepared automatically.

3

Admin Approves

Admins review only the final approval decision instead of doing repetitive manual record handling.

4

Assets Generate

Receipts, member IDs, QR verification records, and notifications are created instantly after approval.

5

Insights Continue

Meetings, reports, reminders, hierarchy tracking, and finance dashboards stay continuously updated.

Role System

Multi-level governance built into the platform

Role-based access ensures every user sees only what they need, while central leadership still gets a complete view.

Super Admin

Full platform control, reporting, finance, CMS, approvals, and configuration.

National / State Admin

Regional governance, approvals, team management, meetings, and activity oversight.

District / Team Managers

Local member handling, attendance, department operations, and team-level communication.

Members

Personal dashboard, payments, receipts, meetings, directory, and digital identity access.
